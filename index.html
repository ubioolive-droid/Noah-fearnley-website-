Noah Fearnley

Actor | Model | Storyteller

About Noah

Originally from Orange, Connecticut, Noah Fearnley is an American actor and model who has quickly made a name for himself in the entertainment industry. A former high school and college football athlete, Noah pivoted his formidable work ethic toward acting and modeling following a career-ending sports injury.

He studied acting in Florida before making the cross-country move to Hollywood, California. Noah is passionate about emotional storytelling and finds deep purpose in connecting with audiences. When he isn't on set, he enjoys staying fit, spending time with his family, and restoring his classic '68 Mustang.
